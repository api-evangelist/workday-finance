Workday introduces Sana for IT Service Management (ITSM) and a Travel Agent, both designed to automate complex workflows that normally force employees to navigate multiple tools and re-enter data across systems. The agents operate on real-time HR and finance data within Workday to resolve IT tickets and manage travel and expenses in a single conversation.
