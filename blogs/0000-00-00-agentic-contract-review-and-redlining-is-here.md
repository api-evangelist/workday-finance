Workday has launched its Contract Negotiation Agent, an AI tool that performs full-document review and automated redlining of contracts. Rather than replacing entire clauses, the agent suggests targeted redlines that achieve the purpose of approved terms while providing clear rationale for each change. This allows legal teams to focus on strategic negotiation goals rather than mechanical drafting tasks.
