Workday's DevCon drew nearly 10,000 attendees, including 1,500 in person in Las Vegas, to learn about the company's open developer platform and agentic AI tools. CTO Gabe Monroy framed the shift as keeping trust while adding speed, with new capabilities aimed at making innovation accessible to both human developers and AI agents.
