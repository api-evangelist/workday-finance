Workday introduces Adaptive Decision Intelligence, an AI-powered platform designed to transform how financial planning teams conduct analysis and decision-making by enabling exploration of data across the entire organization through natural language conversations. The tool maintains governance and auditability while connecting planning data with operational data sources. It addresses the widespread practice of shadow planning in spreadsheets by bringing critical business analysis into a governed, traceable environment.
